## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Yucatán Channel (Spanish: Canal de Yucatán) or the Straits of Yucatán 
> is a strait between Mexico and Cuba. 
> 
> It connects the Yucatán Basin of the Caribbean Sea with the Gulf of Mexico. 
> It is just over 200 kilometres (120 mi) wide 
> and nearly 2,800 metres (9,200 ft) deep at its deepest point near the coast of Cuba.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yucat%C3%A1n%20Channel)
